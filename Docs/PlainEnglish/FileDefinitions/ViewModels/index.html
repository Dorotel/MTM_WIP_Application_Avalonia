??? What Are ViewModels in MTM?

Think of ViewModels as the control panels for different workstations in your manufacturing facility. Each ViewModel manages what information is displayed to operators and handles all the buttons, forms, and interactions for a specific part of the system.

Just like how each workstation has its own control panel with the specific buttons and displays needed for that operation, each ViewModel provides the controls and information displays for different parts of the MTM application.
