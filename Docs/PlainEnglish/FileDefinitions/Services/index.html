?? What Are Services in MTM?

Think of services as the different departments in a manufacturing facility. Just like you have a warehouse department, quality control department, and shipping department, the MTM software has different services that handle specific responsibilities.

Each service specializes in one area of the business and works with the other services to keep the entire manufacturing operation running smoothly.
