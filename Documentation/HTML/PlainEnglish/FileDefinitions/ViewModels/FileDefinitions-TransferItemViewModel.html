Step 1: Source Validation - Verify sufficient stock exists at FROM location
Step 2: Destination Validation - Confirm TO location is valid and accessible
Step 3: Transfer Authorization - User authorizes the movement with quantity and reason
Step 4: Atomic Update - System simultaneously removes from source and adds to destination
Step 5: Audit Documentation - Complete transfer record created with TransactionType.TRANSFER
