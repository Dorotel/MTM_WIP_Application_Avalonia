üè≠ What is the MTM WIP Application?

The MTM WIP Application is a comprehensive inventory management system designed specifically for manufacturing operations.

Think of it as a digital control center for tracking every part, component, and product as it moves through your manufacturing process. Just like a GPS helps you navigate roads, this application helps you navigate your inventory.
